Our Story

Transforming medication identification through advanced AI technology

Founded in 2025, MediSort AI emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where medication errors are eliminated through innovative technology. Our team of healthcare professionals, AI specialists, and software engineers came together with a shared commitment to addressing the challenges of medication identification and information access.

What began as a research project quickly evolved into a comprehensive solution that now serves healthcare providers, pharmacists, patients, and caregivers worldwide. Our proprietary AI algorithms can identify medications with unparalleled accuracy, while our extensive database provides critical information that enhances medication safety.
